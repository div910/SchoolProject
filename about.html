HISTORY...


Ms. Helen Jerwood, a woman of vision founded Queen Mary's School in 1912 with the motive of educating the
girl child. With the initiative and blessings of our Founder Chairman Sir The Rt. Rev. Dr. Karam Masih, new
possibilities were made available which provided the impetus to bring Queen Mary's Northend into existence,
as he laid the foundation stone on 10th October, 2002 with the first session beginning in April 2003.

VISION...


Rt. Rev. Dr. Karam Masih
Founder Chairman

 
It gives me immense pleasure to know about the growth and development of Queen Mary’s
 Northend, Model Town. It is like the growth of a seed to a full grown tree, where the children are
 nurtured and trained to become confident about life.

Since its inception in 2002 and the first session commencing in April, 2003, the school is striving
 for the noble cause of educating the girl child.

I thank Lord Almighty, that we are able to carry forward the vision of Ms. Helen Jerwood.

MISSION...


Queen Mary's family steps forward with the name of Lord and serve the girl child with love and
  tender care.

           We stand affirm on our belief - By Love Serve One Another.

* Our earnest attempt is to make a child’s first step out of her home a happy and enriching

  experience, taking on priority that brain power is balanced with physical, emotional and social well

  - being if a child is truly to flourish.

* Education sets before us the goal where we take care that our children should not only be

    independent  , but also excited and confident about life.

* It is our duty and endeavour to realize the needs of our children and to provide a healthy

  environment   to grow in so that they cherish and spread the fragrance of knowledge everywhere.

* Once the child joins the Queen Mary’s family, we encourage and motivate her to turn every small

  step she takes into a giant leap in the future.
