HISTORY

Ms. Helen Jerwood, a woman of vision founded
Queen Mary's School in 1912 with the motive of
educating the girl child.Founder Chairman Sir
The Rt. Rev. Dr. Karam Masih laid the foundation
stone on 10th October, 2002 with the first
session beginning in April 2003.
